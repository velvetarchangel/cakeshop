About Us

Nestled in the heart of Sunnyside, BakeShop offers the charm and warmth of a European café. We are proudly situated in the historic Vendome Block, a building that has served our neighbourhood for years. We honour this tradition by supporting our community, through the use of local farmers and artisans. From the heart of our kitchen come freshly prepared soups, sandwiches and binge worthy brunches. All pastries are lovingly baked on premise every morning and our fresh bread is baked each day by our sister company Alforno Bakery & Cafe. Indulge in a cup of organic fair trade coffee or a glass of wine. We are open for breakfast, lunch and dinner 7 days a week.

History

As part of the Teatro Group’s family of restaurants, BakeShop upholds our practice of offering only the finest ingredients to our guests. Since the opening of our flagship restaurant in 1993, we have been proud members of Calgary’s vibrant downtown culinary scene. Our philosophy is aimed towards enhancing the culture of our community through food, wine and support of the arts, all the while preserving our neighbourhood’s rich history. Happily serving you Monday to Friday 7am to 9pm, Saturday and Sunday 8am to 9pm, Holidays 8am to 5pm.
